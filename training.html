Why train

The same authority Jesus gave His followers, He still gives today.

Deliverance is not reserved for a select few. Jesus equipped ordinary believers to set the captives free — and the need has never been greater. Yet many sincere Christians have never been shown how to recognise bondage or minister freedom safely.

Our training gives your church or team a clear, biblical, and compassionate foundation — so you can minister with confidence rather than fear, and care for people well.

What you'll learn

A complete, grounded foundation for ministry.

01

Biblical foundations

Understand what scripture actually teaches about deliverance, authority, and the believer's identity in Christ.

02

Recognising the signs

Learn to discern spiritual bondage from natural struggles with wisdom, humility, and care — never assumption.

03

Ministering freedom

Walk through how a session unfolds — praying with authority, leading someone gently, and responding to what arises.

04

Safety & aftercare

Protect those you serve and yourself — confidentiality, boundaries, follow-up, and helping people stay free.
